RENOVATION

Home renovation is the process of updating, improving, or transforming an existing residential space. This typically involves changes to enhance functionality, aesthetics, or both. Renovations can range from small-scale updates to extensive overhauls. Key steps in a home renovation include assessing the current condition, setting a budget, obtaining necessary permits, making structural or cosmetic changes, upgrading utilities, installing new features, and adding final touches.
